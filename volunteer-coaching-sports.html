Sports & Operations Volunteer Opportunities

Home / Volunteerism / Sports & Operations Volunteer Opportunities

Volunteers are the lifeblood of the Mathews-Dickey Boys' & Girls' Club. Without the thousands of individuals who lend their time and talents each year, the Club could not continue its important youth service mission. Providing specialized service opportunities is a key to the success of the Club's volunteer program. The following are volunteer administration opportunities:

GIRLS' COACH (BASEBALL, BASKETBALL OR FOOTBALL)

Responsibilities

  • Teach the fundamentals of sports
  • Provide positive directions for youth
  • Teach players how to communicate with others and succeed, on and off the field/court
  • Assist volunteers, parents and members involved in program

Requirements

  • Ability to work with children, parents and adult volunteers
  • Organizational and leadership skills
  • Ability to motivate youth and organize youth activities

Hours

  • Weekdays - 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m (Hours may vary)
  • Saturdays - 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. (Hours may vary)


GROUNDSKEEPER

Responsibilities

  • Maintain upkeep of the grounds
  • Operate riding lawnmower

Requirements

  • Knowledge of landscaping
  • Ability to operate riding lawnmower
  • Remove debris from campus ground

Hours

  • Weekdays - 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.
  • Saturdays - 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.


LIFEGUARD

Responsibilities

  • Supervise recreational swimming
  • Teach swimming to Club members

Requirements

  • CPR certification
  • USI certification
  • Desire to work with children

Hours

  • Weekdays - 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.
  • Saturdays - 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.


PROGRAM ASSISTANT

Responsibilities

  • Monitor program activities
  • Enter and maintain membership data
  • Interact with and provide customer service for volunteers
  • Help coordinate special projects
  • Recruit volunteers for special activities

Requirements

  • Ability to work with children
  • Ability to develop programming ideas

Hours

  • Weekdays - 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m.
  • Saturdays - 9 a.m. to 2 p.m.


STADIUM CONCESSION PERSONNEL

Responsibilities

  • Assist in serving customers
  • Prepare/serve food and beverages to players/parents
  • Assist with stocking inventory

Requirements

  • Ability to count money
  • Enjoy working with people
  • Desire to work with children

Hours

  • Weekdays - 5:30 p.m. to 7:30 p.m. (Hours may vary)
  • Saturdays - 9 a.m. to 2 p.m. (Hours may vary)

Mathews-Dickey Boys' & Girls' Club is a nonprofit 501 (c) (3) United Way member agency that annually provides educational, athletic and cultural enrichment programs to more than 40,000 young people from throughout the St. Louis metropolitan area. For 52 years the Club has taught youth respect, restraint and responsibility so that they may obtain credibility, integrity and accountability. For more information, call Barbara A.
